# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and empathetic
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
